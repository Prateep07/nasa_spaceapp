Goal 13: Climate Action

Overview: Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Key Targets:

  • Strengthen resilience and adaptive capacity to climate-related hazards and natural disasters in all countries.
  • Integrate climate change measures into national policies, strategies, and planning.
  • Improve education, awareness, and human and institutional capacity on climate change mitigation, adaptation, and impact reduction.
  • Mobilize significant financial resources from various sources to support climate action in developing countries.
  • Promote mechanisms for raising capacity for effective climate change-related planning and management in least developed countries.

Importance of Goal 13:

Climate change poses a significant threat to our planet, impacting ecosystems, weather patterns, and human livelihoods. Addressing climate change is crucial for ensuring sustainable development, protecting our natural resources, and safeguarding the future of our planet. Goal 13 emphasizes the need for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts on all aspects of life.
