Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation

Overview: Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.

Key Targets:

  • By 2030, achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all.
  • By 2030, achieve access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene facilities for all, with a focus on vulnerable populations.
  • Improve water quality by reducing pollution, minimizing the release of hazardous chemicals and materials, and substantially increasing water-use efficiency across all sectors.
  • Protect and restore water-related ecosystems, such as rivers, wetlands, and lakes, to contribute to water quality and availability.
  • Support local communities in improving water and sanitation management.

Importance of Goal 6:

Access to clean water and sanitation is essential for public health, economic development, and the environment. Ensuring that everyone has access to these resources helps prevent waterborne diseases, supports food security, and enhances the quality of life. Sustainable water management is crucial for maintaining healthy ecosystems and combating climate change.
