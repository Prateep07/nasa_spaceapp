Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Overview: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns.

Key Targets:

  • Implement the 10-Year Framework of Programmes on Sustainable Consumption and Production.
  • Reduce global per capita food waste at the retail and consumer levels.
  • Encourage industries, businesses, and consumers to recycle and reduce waste.
  • Support developing countries to move towards more sustainable patterns of consumption.
  • Develop and implement tools to monitor sustainable development impacts for sustainable tourism.

Importance of Goal 12:

Responsible consumption and production are essential for sustainable development. Our planet has limited resources, and if we continue to exploit them unsustainably, we risk depleting them and causing irreversible damage to the environment. This goal aims to change the way we consume and produce goods, making our societies more sustainable and helping to conserve our natural resources.
