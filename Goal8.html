Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth

Overview: Promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all.

Key Targets:

  • Achieve higher levels of economic productivity through diversification, technological upgrading, and innovation.
  • Promote development-oriented policies that encourage entrepreneurship and job creation.
  • Achieve full and productive employment and decent work for all women and men, including for young people and persons with disabilities.
  • Decent work for youth is a priority, and the target is to increase the youth employment rate by 2020.
  • Develop and operationalize a global strategy for youth employment.

Importance of Goal 8:

Decent work and economic growth are crucial for reducing poverty and inequality. Promoting sustained economic growth creates job opportunities and empowers individuals to improve their living standards. Achieving this goal contributes to social stability and the well-being of communities, making it essential for overall sustainable development.
