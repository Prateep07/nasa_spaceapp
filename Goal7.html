Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy

Overview: Ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all.

Key Targets:

  • By 2030, ensure universal access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy services.
  • Increase substantially the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix by 2030.
  • Double the global rate of improvement in energy efficiency by 2030.
  • Enhance international cooperation to facilitate access to clean energy research and technology.
  • Promote investment in energy infrastructure and clean energy technology.

Importance of Goal 7:

Access to affordable and clean energy is essential for sustainable development and is crucial for reducing poverty and improving living standards. Clean energy solutions not only combat climate change but also provide economic opportunities, create jobs, and improve health outcomes. Achieving this goal is vital for fostering resilient infrastructure and promoting inclusive and sustainable industrialization.
