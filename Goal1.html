Goal 1: No Poverty

Overview: End poverty in all its forms everywhere.

Key Targets:

  • Ensure that all men, women, and children, in particular the poor and vulnerable, have equal rights to economic resources.
  • Build the resilience of those living in vulnerable situations.
  • Ensure significant mobilization of resources from various sources.

Importance of Goal 1:

Poverty eradication is essential for sustainable development. It affects all aspects of human life and limits the ability to make meaningful contributions to society. Without addressing poverty, achieving other Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is nearly impossible.
