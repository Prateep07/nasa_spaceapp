Goal 10: Reduced Inequalities

Overview: Reduce inequality within and among countries.

Key Targets:

  • Reduce the income inequality among the bottom 40% of the population.
  • Ensure equal opportunity and reduce inequalities of outcome, including by eliminating discriminatory laws, policies, and practices.
  • Empower and promote the social, economic, and political inclusion of all, irrespective of age, gender, disability, race, ethnicity, origin, religion, economic or other status.
  • Improve the regulation and monitoring of financial markets and institutions to encourage development assistance and foreign direct investment to regions where the need is greatest.
  • Encourage development assistance and foreign direct investment to regions where the need is greatest.

Importance of Goal 10:

Reducing inequality is essential for achieving sustainable development. Inequality hinders economic growth, generates social unrest, and perpetuates poverty. By promoting inclusivity and equal opportunities, we can ensure that everyone benefits from economic growth and development, leading to more resilient societies and a better quality of life for all.
