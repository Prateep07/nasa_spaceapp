Goal 5: Gender Equality

Overview: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls.

Key Targets:

  • End all forms of discrimination against all women and girls everywhere.
  • Eliminate all forms of violence against all women and girls in public and private spheres, including trafficking and sexual and other types of exploitation.
  • Ensure that women have full and effective participation and equal opportunities for leadership at all levels of decision-making in political, economic, and public life.
  • Ensure universal access to sexual and reproductive health and reproductive rights.
  • Undertake reforms to give women equal rights to economic resources, as well as access to ownership and control over land and other forms of property.

Importance of Goal 5:

Gender equality is essential for sustainable development and the achievement of all Sustainable Development Goals. Empowering women and girls leads to more inclusive societies, drives economic growth, and ensures the well-being of future generations.
