Goal 17: Partnerships for the Goals

Overview: Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

Key Targets:

  • Finance: Mobilize additional financial resources for developing countries from multiple sources.
  • Technology: Promote the development, transfer, dissemination, and diffusion of environmentally sound technologies to developing countries.
  • Capacity Building: Enhance international support for implementing effective and targeted capacity-building in developing countries.
  • Trade: Promote a universal, rules-based, open, non-discriminatory, and equitable multilateral trading system.
  • Data, Monitoring, and Accountability: Encourage and promote effective public, public-private, and civil society partnerships.

Importance of Goal 17:

Achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) requires strong partnerships between governments, the private sector, and civil society. Collaborations and partnerships are essential for pooling resources, sharing knowledge, and driving innovation. Goal 17 emphasizes the importance of multi-stakeholder partnerships in fostering sustainable development and ensuring that no one is left behind.
