Goal 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions

Overview: Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions at all levels.

Key Targets:

  • Significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere.
  • Promote the rule of law at the national and international levels and ensure equal access to justice for all.
  • Develop effective, accountable, and transparent institutions at all levels.
  • Ensure responsive, inclusive, participatory, and representative decision-making at all levels.
  • Strengthen the participation of developing countries in the institutions of global governance.

Importance of Goal 16:

Peace, justice, and strong institutions are essential for sustainable development. Without peace, societies cannot flourish, and without justice, rights cannot be protected. Strong institutions are crucial for promoting the rule of law and ensuring that everyone has access to justice. This goal emphasizes the importance of building effective, accountable institutions to foster inclusive societies and sustainable development.
