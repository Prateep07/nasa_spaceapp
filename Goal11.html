Goal 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities

Overview: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable.

Key Targets:

  • Ensure access to adequate, safe, and affordable housing and basic services for all.
  • Provide access to safe, affordable, accessible, and sustainable transport systems for all.
  • Enhance inclusive and sustainable urbanization, particularly for the marginalized and vulnerable.
  • Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities, including air quality and waste management.
  • Protect and safeguard the world’s cultural and natural heritage.

Importance of Goal 11:

Urbanization is inevitable, but if cities continue to develop in unsustainable ways, they will become breeding grounds for inequality, pollution, and social unrest. Sustainable urban planning is not just about buildings or infrastructure; it's about creating cities that are inclusive, environmentally responsible, and resilient to shocks. Prioritizing sustainability in cities is not optional—it's a necessity if we want to avoid environmental collapse and societal breakdown.
